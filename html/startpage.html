Your #1 Arena Online Multiplayer Game

Naruto-Arena (2006-2018) is an online multiplayer strategy game, where you play as 153 characters in total. The game is no longer available. In this project, I'm aiming to bring back a bit of the memories i've had as a child enjoying this beautiful game.
